About D-Code

D-Code is platform originally established under Progate Week of Learning Program JKLU Chapter. The theme of this website is building a platform which is user-friendly, attractive and easy to use. It is suitable for every group starting from new comers to professional coders.

The website is currently on its version 1.0.0 started on 18.02.2020, D-Code is currently a static website trying its best to give proper and genuine knowledge to its user. Although it focuses developing with every passing day and introducing more languages and advance features to the platform making it more friendly and powerful.
